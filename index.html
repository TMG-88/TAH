Hi all my Buddies!

The Thrilling Adventure Hour (TAH) is a stage show and podcast in the style of old time radio created by Messrs. Acker and Blacker, directed by Aaron Ginsburg, with music by the Andy Paley Orchestra, featuring The WorkJuice Players, and brought to you by WorkJuice Coffee, Patriot Brand Cigarettes, and Sweetie-Pie Cakes.

TAH began as a live stage show at M Bar in Los Angeles on March 5, 2005. In 2010, the show moved to Largo at the Coronet and began being recorded for a weekly podcast, which launched in January 2011. The show joined the Nerdist podcasting network in October 2011. While the main run of the podcast ended in April 2015, there are still occasional live shows and podcast releases.

For all up-to-the-minute news about the show, follow @ThrillingAdv, check ThrillingAdventureHour on Facebook, or visit the Official Website.
