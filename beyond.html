Beyond Belief is a show about two married mediums who drink a lot. A lot.

Trading quips and tossing back cocktails, the Doyles take what they want and hoodwink their clientele for the rest. Ghosts, vampires, werewolves, mummies, even diabolical gingerbreadmen are nothing but obstacles in the way of the liquor cabinet for our fast-talking, hard-drinking sleuths.

They are, however, afraid of bees. It's generally best not to talk about that time a bee got in here.
